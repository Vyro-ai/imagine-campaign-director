# Imagine.Art Workflow Prompt Pack

## Starting Inputs

- Product photo: smoked glass black orchid perfume bottle
- Mood board: clinical room, metal tray, white tile, condensation, abrupt sound hits

## Creative Direction

Selected spine: A fragrance bottle is treated like clinical evidence; its sound arrives before its image, and the full product is withheld until the room goes quiet.

Rejected premise notes:

- Blue-hour nocturne lost because wet pavement and petals were expected.
- Romantic human trace lost because it made the bottle a prop in another hand shot.
- Product monument lost because the brief needed more sequence and tension.

Direction DNA: Jonathan Glazer clinical distance translated into cool observation, precise blocking, quiet threat, and abrupt sonic punctuation.

Controlled flaw: bottle is partially blocked by a metal tray edge until the final hit.

Aesthetic refusals: no wet pavement, no orchid petals, no smoke swirl, no centered bottle before the final hit.

## Workflow Sections

1. `SOURCE / Inputs`
2. `ANALYSIS / Product + Sound`
3. `MUSIC / Stopper Click Beat Map`
4. `STRATEGY / Clinical Evidence`
5. `STILLS / Lab Anchors`
6. `STORYBOARD / Beat-Led Reveals`
7. `MOTION / Evidence Sequence`
8. `EDIT / Assembly`
9. `TYPE / End Card`
10. `QC / Review + Rejects`
11. `EXPORT / 16x9 Final`

## Music Studio Prompt

Create a 15-second royalty-free sound-first fragrance cue. Music use: sound-led spine. Genre: minimal clinical electronic with object percussion. Mood: cool, controlled, tense, expensive by precision rather than romance. Tempo: sparse 70 BPM pulse. Instruments: glass stopper click, metal tray ring, soft sub pulse, one dry snare, narrow high shimmer, silence. Vocal direction: instrumental. Beat ladder: stopper click opens before product is seen; sub enters when bottle edge appears; dry snare hits on lab-scale needle twitch; music drops out before the full bottle reveal; one high shimmer after final product isolation. Avoid wet-pavement romance, lush strings, smoky lounge mood, horror sting, famous artist imitation.

