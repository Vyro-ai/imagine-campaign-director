## Stillframe Prompts

### SF / Bottle Hook

Smoked glass black orchid perfume bottle on rain-dark stone pavement at blue hour, black orchid petals nearby, narrow warm rim light on the bottle edge, shallow reflection, restrained luxury fragrance campaign, clean negative space for typography, 16:9, no text, no logos.

### SF / Macro Cap

Extreme macro of smoked glass perfume cap with condensation beads and warm rim light, dark blue-hour background falloff, tactile glass and metal detail, no text, no logos.

### SF / Final Product Plate

Clean product-first end-card plate, smoked glass perfume bottle centered slightly low, dark wet stone surface, soft blue shadow, warm edge highlight, large clean negative space above for deterministic typography.

## Motion Prompts

### MV / 10s Nocturne

Node duration setting: 10.

Prompt: Seedance campaign clip from approved stillframes. Opening phase: slow push toward the bottle reflection on wet pavement. Phase 2: a black-gloved hand passes softly behind the bottle without touching it. Phase 3: orchid petals shift in a narrow warm light strip. Phase 4: macro cap detail catches a moving highlight. Final hold: locked product hold with clean negative space for end-card typography.

Negative: no fake text, no invented logo, no deformed bottle, no broken hand anatomy, no excessive smoke, no chaotic camera.

## Music Studio Prompt

Create a 15-second royalty-free instrumental campaign bed for a black orchid fragrance film. Mood is nocturnal, restrained, tactile, and expensive. Genre: cinematic electronic ambient. Tempo: slow pulse around 72 BPM. Instruments: soft sub bass, glassy synth texture, distant low strings, minimal brushed percussion, one warm shimmer hit for the final product hold. No vocals. Avoid upbeat pop, busy drums, horror tone, and famous artist imitation.

## Edit Notes

Add product name and CTA in the end-card layer. Keep type minimal and high contrast. Cut final hold to the music shimmer.
