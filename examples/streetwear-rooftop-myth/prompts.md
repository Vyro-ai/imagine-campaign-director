## Stillframe Prompts

### SF / Flash Hook

Concrete rooftop at night, model wearing black technical jacket with subtle reflective piping, urban skyline behind, hard camera flash, wind lifting jacket hem, rebellious posture, low angle, 16:9, no text, no logos.

### SF / Garment Detail

Macro jacket zipper and reflective piping on black technical fabric, rain beads on concrete, flash edge light, no text, no logos.

### SF / CTA Hold

Back-view jacket silhouette against night skyline, empty sky and concrete negative space for deterministic drop date typography, hard flash falloff, 16:9.

## Motion Prompts

### MV / Rooftop Myth

Node duration setting: 10.

Prompt: Imagine.Art streetwear drop clip. Opening phase: flash reveals the subject stepping into frame. Phase 2: wind lifts the jacket hem and reflective piping catches light. Phase 3: low-angle portrait hold with hands in pockets. Phase 4: macro zipper and wet concrete detail. Final hold: subject turns away into final silhouette with clean sky for CTA.

Negative: no fake logos, no extra fingers, no broken gait, no unreadable signage, no warped zipper.

## Music Studio Prompt

Create a 15-second royalty-free instrumental bed for a night rooftop streetwear drop. Genre: minimal trap electronic. Mood: confident, sharp, nocturnal, rebellious. Tempo: medium, around 92 BPM. Instruments: sparse drums, sub bass, short synth stabs, camera-flash impact hits, subtle wind texture. No vocals. Avoid cheerful pop, crowded percussion, and famous artist imitation.

## Edit Notes

Use one flash accent on the first cut. Add drop date and CTA in post, not inside generated video.
