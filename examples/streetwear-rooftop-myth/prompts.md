# Imagine.Art Workflow Prompt Pack

## Starting Inputs

- Product photo: black technical jacket
- Brief: limited streetwear drop

## Creative Direction

Selected spine: A black technical jacket passes through an ordinary parking-garage stairwell like observed evidence, not a performance, leaving the viewer with an empty safety mirror after the drop has already moved on.

Rejected premise notes:

- Default restraint lost because it made another polished black-jacket portrait.
- Flash-streetwear lost because rooftop, skyline, wind, and posture are already overused.
- Sound-first was kept as support, but the visual idea is stronger as a single-take observation.

Direction DNA: Frederick Wiseman observational procedure translated into fixed institutional framing, practical fluorescent light, behavior over pose, and no stylized rebellion.

Controlled flaw: crop cuts the top of the head and the subject never gives camera a clean hero pose.

Aesthetic refusals: no rooftop, no skyline, no flash, no wind-on-fabric, no rebellious posture.

## Stillframe Prompts

### SF / Stairwell Anchor

```text
SHOT: Medium-wide observational film still through a parking garage stairwell safety mirror.
SUBJECT: Adult model wearing the locked black technical jacket fully zipped, hands at sides, weight on right leg, looking down the stairwell at something off-camera, not posing.
ENVIRONMENT: Concrete parking garage stairwell, level B2 paint on wall, cracked convex safety mirror, ceiling vent haze, late afternoon institutional quiet.
LIGHTING: Single overhead fluorescent practical, green-shifted, no fill, weak reflective piping visibility at the jacket edge.
CAMERA: ARRI Alexa 35 look, 35mm spherical lens, f/4, eye-level with the third stair from the top, locked-off.
FILM STOCK / FORMAT: Digital observational documentary frame with fine grain.
COLOR GRADE: Muted concrete gray, sickly green highlights, low saturation, honest shadows.
MOOD: procedural, unsentimental, observed streetwear drop.
COMPOSITION: Safety mirror upper-left, landing lower-right, subject partially clipped by doorframe, negative space in the empty stairwell.
ASPECT RATIO: 9:16 delivery with protected center crop.
TEXTURE: Fluorescent flicker feel, concrete pores, nylon jacket texture, slight mirror distortion.
IMPERFECTION: Crop cuts the top of the model's head and the jacket is only partly flattered by the light.
AESTHETIC REFUSALS: no rooftop, no skyline, no flash, no wind-on-fabric, no rebellious posture.
AVOID: plastic skin, smoothed faces, oversharpening, HDR look, extra fingers, distorted hands, garbled text, cartoon style, illustration, fake logos, readable signage, broken walking gait.
```

## Motion Prompts

### MV / Stairwell Oner

Node duration setting: 15.

Prompt:

```text
Subject: observational streetwear campaign in a concrete parking garage stairwell. The locked black technical jacket is the only product truth; performance is unaware of camera.
Reference roles: @Image1 controls jacket geometry and material; @Image2 controls stairwell environment, mirror placement, fluorescent light, and final empty-frame composition.
Phase ladder: Opening phase: locked frame on the cracked safety mirror and empty landing. Phase 2: the model's reflection enters from below while climbing the last stairs. Phase 3: model arrives on the landing, turns one-quarter toward the mirror, and does not look at it. Phase 4: hold the stillness long enough for one fluorescent flicker. Final hold: model exits camera-right; the empty mirror and empty landing remain.
Camera: locked medium-wide eye-level frame, no push, no orbit, no handheld energy.
Continuity: preserve jacket silhouette, stairwell geometry, green fluorescent color, and mirror distortion.
Aesthetic refusals: no rooftop, no skyline, no flash, no wind-on-fabric, no rebellious posture.
Constraints: no fake logos, no readable signage, no broken gait, no extra fingers, no identity drift, no chaotic camera, no generated typography.
```

## Music Studio Prompt

Create a 15-second royalty-free sound-led bed for an observational streetwear single take. Music use: background score. Genre: near-silent room tone with dry object percussion. Mood: patient, unsentimental, concrete, tense without aggression. Tempo: irregular footsteps around 76 BPM, not a beat grid. Instruments: fluorescent buzz, distant HVAC, three dry stair footsteps, one low sub pulse only while the jacket is fully visible, final return to room tone. Vocal direction: instrumental. Beat ladder: buzz opens; footsteps introduce subject; sub pulse enters at landing; one fluorescent click during hold; silence after exit. Avoid trap drums, camera flash hits, heroic risers, rebellious swagger, famous artist imitation.

Add drop date and CTA in deterministic type after the empty-frame hold. Do not add montage inserts or product macros; the single take is the campaign.
