# Director's Treatment

Campaign thesis: The jacket earns attention by passing through an ordinary institutional space without asking for it.

Selected creative spine: A black technical jacket moves through a parking garage stairwell in one observed take until the wearer exits, leaving the safety mirror empty.

Rejected premises: Rooftop flash was rejected as cliche. Default restraint was rejected as another black-jacket portrait. Sound-first was retained as room tone rather than the spine.

Direction DNA: Frederick Wiseman observational procedure translated into locked framing, institutional light, unperformed behavior, and patient duration.

Controlled flaw: The crop cuts the model's eye-line and the fluorescent light is slightly unflattering.

Aesthetic refusals: no rooftop, no skyline, no flash, no wind-on-fabric, no rebellious posture.

Music spine: fluorescent buzz, dry footsteps, one sub pulse during full jacket visibility, silence after exit.

Seedance plan: one 15s Seedance 2 node, reference-image set mode, no internal cuts, final hold on the empty mirror.
