# Brief

## Campaign

- Brand: Noctivelle Paris
- Product: Noir Orchid Eau de Parfum
- Title: The Night Belongs to You
- Category: luxury fragrance / fashion editorial / nocturnal beauty
- Primary format: 9:16 vertical social hero ad
- Secondary format: 16:9 cinematic cutdown
- Duration: 10 seconds
- CTA: Discover Noir Orchid

## Objective

Make Noir Orchid feel intimate, expensive, cinematic, restrained, and unforgettable. The video should sell the feeling of the fragrance before revealing the product as the final object of the mood.

## Production Profile

Fragrance.

This profile protects bottle geometry, cap shape, label truth, glass thickness, and final packshot reliability. It does not define the film's creative structure. Avoid treating the category as automatic wet pavement, orchids, smoke, or generic nocturnal romance unless the treatment earns those choices.

## Emotional Temperature

Cool nocturnal atmosphere with warm skin and gold accents. Composed, sensual, self-possessed, low volume, and precise.

## Visual Palette

- Near black for background and packaging shadow.
- Orchid wine and muted violet for floral depth and wardrobe undertone.
- Warm gold for cap edge, label frame, logo/type accents, and practical light hits.
- Soft parchment for optional deterministic end-card contrast only.
- Cool slate for mist, rain, stone, city reflections.

## Automation Boundary

- GPT Image 2 inside Imagine.Art: storyboard panels, key stills, product plates, material macros, end-card plate.
- Seedance inside Imagine.Art: motion clips from approved storyboard panels.
- Imagine.Art Music Studio: original instrumental score.
- HyperFrames/editing: final typography, logo placement, product name, CTA, cut timing, crop versions, caption-safe layout, and final export.
- Human footage: not required.

## Six-Shot Structure

| Shot | Time | Job | Automation |
|---|---:|---|---|
| 01 | 0.0-1.2s | Hook: black orchid and wet stone catch one gold reflection | GPT Image 2 panel, Seedance motion |
| 02 | 1.2-2.6s | Subject appears on rain-dark balcony or street, not posing hard | GPT Image 2 panel, Seedance motion |
| 03 | 2.6-4.0s | Skin/silk/detail, bottle edge barely present | GPT Image 2 panel, Seedance motion |
| 04 | 4.0-5.8s | Quiet city movement through amber-lit corridor/street | GPT Image 2 panel, Seedance motion |
| 05 | 5.8-7.8s | Product ritual: bottle near collarbone or placed on black stone | GPT Image 2 panel, Seedance motion |
| 06 | 7.8-10.0s | Locked final product memory image with deterministic type | GPT Image 2 clean plate, Seedance optional micro-motion, HyperFrames type |

## Main Risks

- Product label text will be unreliable in generated video.
- Bottle geometry, black faceted cap, gold frame, and label placement may drift between shots.
- Hands near glass can create anatomy and contact failures.
- Wet reflections can break physical plausibility.
- Overusing orchids, smoke, neon, or slow motion will make the spot generic.

## Execution Rule

Do not present the workflow as finished until source assets are uploaded, storyboard panels are generated and approved, Seedance clips are generated from approved panels, the Music Studio track is generated, QC is complete, and the final edit uses only reviewed motion outputs.
