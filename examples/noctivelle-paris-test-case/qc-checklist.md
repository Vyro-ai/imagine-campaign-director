# QC Checklist

Status options: `pass`, `pass with minor fixes`, `needs revision`, `reject`.

## Pre-Generation Gate

- [ ] Source zip assets uploaded into `SOURCE / Inputs`.
- [ ] Asset roles documented.
- [ ] Product truth is separated from style/mood references.
- [ ] GPT Image 2 storyboard panel prompts are loaded.
- [ ] Seedance nodes are not connected to unreviewed first-pass stills.
- [ ] Music Studio prompt exists before final assembly.
- [ ] Final workflow plan contains only intended used nodes.

## Stillframe Review

Score each approved GPT Image 2 panel from 1-5:

- [ ] Hook strength.
- [ ] Product/world relevance.
- [ ] Brand palette control.
- [ ] Visual hierarchy.
- [ ] Product/bottle continuity where applicable.
- [ ] Hands/anatomy where applicable.
- [ ] No fake text, logos, watermarks, or accidental signage.
- [ ] Crop safety for 9:16.
- [ ] Negative space for final typography on Panel 06.

Hard reject stillframes with warped hands, unreadable fake label dominance, plastic skin, bright e-commerce lighting, overstuffed flowers, random smoke, or inconsistent bottle geometry.

## Motion Review

Score each selected Seedance clip from 1-5:

- [ ] Motion serves a specific editorial job.
- [ ] Camera movement is restrained and physically plausible.
- [ ] Product shape, cap, label frame, and material stay consistent.
- [ ] Hands remain simple and believable.
- [ ] Wet reflections and glass highlights behave plausibly.
- [ ] No chaotic orbit, fast zoom, or unmotivated slow motion.
- [ ] No fake text, accidental logo, or watermark.
- [ ] Cut point has a usable start and end frame.

Hard reject motion clips with product drift, broken physical contact, extra fingers, melted glass, accidental signage, or a final frame that cannot hold typography.

## Music Review

- [ ] Track is instrumental.
- [ ] Mood is nocturnal and restrained, not horror or nightclub.
- [ ] Broad hit points support 0.0, 1.2, 2.6, 4.0, 5.8, and 7.8s cuts.
- [ ] Final 2 seconds can support product hold and CTA.
- [ ] No famous artist imitation or recognizable melody.

## Edit Review

- [ ] Six-shot structure fits exactly 10 seconds.
- [ ] Opening hook lands in first second.
- [ ] Product is teased before full reveal.
- [ ] Shot shapes vary: macro, portrait, detail, city, ritual, product plate.
- [ ] Final product frame is memorable and legible.
- [ ] Typography is deterministic and not generated into the footage.
- [ ] CTA is clear.
- [ ] No captions or type collide with face, product, or bottle label.
- [ ] 9:16 crop is primary and polished.
- [ ] 16:9 cutdown does not feel like an afterthought.

## Target Score Before Final Export

Minimum average: 4.0 / 5.

Required 5/5 areas for this test case:

- Imagine.Art workflow completeness.
- Product closeout strength.
- Typography safety.
- Product continuity.

Anything below 3/5 in product clarity, artifact control, or final memory image requires revision before export.
