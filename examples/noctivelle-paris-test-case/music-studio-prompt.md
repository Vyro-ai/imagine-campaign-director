# Imagine.Art Music Studio Prompt

Create a 10-second royalty-free instrumental campaign bed for a luxury nocturnal fragrance film called "Noir Orchid" by the fictional brand Noctivelle Paris.

Mood: restrained, sensual, composed, mysterious, expensive, nocturnal, cool shadows with warm gold highlights.

Genre/style: cinematic electronic ambient with a minimal fashion-film pulse.

Tempo: slow pulse around 68-74 BPM, not dance music.

Instrumentation: soft sub bass pulse, low brushed percussion, distant muted strings, glassy synth shimmer, subtle reversed cymbal breath, one warm gold shimmer hit for the final product reveal.

Vocal direction: no vocals, no spoken words.

Duration target: 10 seconds, with a clean final 2-second hold.

Edit hit points:

- 0.0s: immediate low shimmer and soft impact for orchid reflection hook.
- 1.2s: restrained first cut accent for subject reveal.
- 2.6s: quiet texture accent for skin/silk detail.
- 4.0s: low pulse deepens for rain city movement.
- 5.8s: warm shimmer rises for product ritual reveal.
- 7.8s: final elegant hit into locked product frame.
- 8.4-10.0s: sustained final tone under brand/product/CTA typography.

Avoid: upbeat pop, famous artist imitation, horror stingers, nightclub energy, busy trap hats, loud drums, vocals, romantic piano cliche, overdramatic trailer booms, chaotic risers.

The music is the edit spine. Generate the Music Studio track before final motion selection when possible, then align hard cuts to the broad hit points above.
