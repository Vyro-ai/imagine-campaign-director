# Director's Treatment

Campaign thesis: Beauty becomes more interesting when the perfect face is withheld and the object carries the power.

Selected creative spine: A chrome compact fractures a partial eye reflection until the product becomes a small silver monument in black space.

Rejected premises: Glossy beauty was rejected as mirror-demo stock. Soft portrait intimacy was rejected because it depended on face perfection. Lab-test procedural was rejected because it drained the compact of desire.

Direction DNA: Hiro product-monument grammar translated into hard rim, black surround, severe negative space, and object hierarchy.

Controlled flaw: Mid-blink partial reflection, clipped face, and visible chrome dust stay in the frame by choice.

Aesthetic refusals: no poreless skin, no perfect mirror symmetry, no slow push, no centered compact before closeout.

Music spine: metallic tick, tight kick, glass bend, low reveal hit, then warm pad with ticks removed.

Seedance plan: two Seedance 2 nodes, reference-image set mode, minimal lateral motion, deterministic type only in edit.
