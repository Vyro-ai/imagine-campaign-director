# Imagine.Art Workflow Prompt Pack

## Starting Inputs

- Product photo: chrome compact
- Style image: reflective studio beauty lighting
- Mood board: precise makeup, chrome panels, extreme close-ups

## Creative Direction

Selected spine: A chrome compact withholds the beauty face through broken reflections and hard rim light until the object becomes a small monument in black space.

Rejected premise notes:

- Glossy electronic beauty lost because it became a clean mirror demo.
- Soft portrait intimacy lost because it over-relied on face perfection.
- Wrong-genre lab test lost because the product needed more desire than procedure.

Direction DNA: Hiro product-monument grammar translated into hard rim, black surround, object isolation, and severe negative space.

Controlled flaw: mid-blink partial reflection and a crop that cuts the clean beauty face.

Aesthetic refusals: no poreless skin, no perfect mirror symmetry, no slow push, no centered compact before closeout.
