# Campaign Archetypes

## Product Hero

Best for launches and premium goods. Structure: hook, product reveal, material proof, use moment, final product-first CTA.

## Editorial Film

Best for fashion, beauty, and lifestyle. Structure: iconic opener, world build, movement, detail accent, emotional pause, final hold.

## Editorial Nocturne

Best default for vague luxury fashion prompts aimed at Gen Z when no brand assets are supplied. Structure: silhouette hook, nocturnal world reveal, material/detail accent, controlled model movement, final elegant hold. Use one model, restrained styling, rain-wet concrete/glass/chrome, cool shadows, warm practical light, no logos, and no text unless requested.

## Fragrance Nocturne

Best for fragrance and mood-led products. Structure: atmosphere, bottle detail, human trace, sensory metaphor, final packshot.

## Streetwear Drop

Best for apparel drops. Structure: subject entrance, attitude portrait, garment detail, group/world cue, flash product frame, drop CTA.

## Beauty Precision

Best for makeup, skincare, and editorial beauty. Structure: skin/light hook, macro texture, application or transformation, mirror/portrait, product closeout.

## Chrome Product

Best for tech, cosmetics, accessories, and design objects. Structure: reflective hook, object geometry, surface detail, controlled motion, clean end card.
