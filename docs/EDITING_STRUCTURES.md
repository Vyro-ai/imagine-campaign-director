# Editing Structures

## 10-Second Campaign

Use about four major phases:

1. 0-2s: hook
2. 2-5s: world or product reveal
3. 5-8s: detail/action accent
4. 8-10s: final hero hold

## 20-Second Campaign

1. hook
2. world reveal
3. product reveal
4. movement or use moment
5. macro proof
6. emotional pause
7. CTA

## Beat-Led Edit

Use major beats for cuts, reveals, flashes, and final holds. Do not shake or flash on every beat.

## Product Closeout

Keep the final card product-first. Generate the clean product image without baked-in text, then add brand/product typography in the edit.
