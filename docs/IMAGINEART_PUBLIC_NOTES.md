# Imagine.Art Public Notes

These notes summarize public Imagine.Art product behavior used by this repo. They are not private internal guidance.

## Workflows

Public Imagine.Art pages describe Workflows as node-based creative workflows that can generate image, video, and audio variations, use text/images/video files as inputs, support subtitles/transitions/effects/background music, and organize scene planning on a drag-and-drop canvas.

Relevant public pages:

- https://www.imagine.art/workflow
- https://help.imagine.art/workflows/run-your-first-workflow

## Models

Public Imagine.Art pages mention workflow model options including Nano Banana Pro, Seedance 2, Kling, and multimodal text models available through AI Copilot-style nodes.

Relevant public page:

- https://docs.imagine.art/workflows/ai-models

## Music Studio

Public Imagine.Art pages describe Music Studio as supporting text/song prompts, lyrics, genre/style/instrument/mood customization, generation, and download. The public Music Studio page states that image-to-music is not currently available, so visual inputs should be translated into text-based music direction.

Relevant public page:

- https://www.imagine.art/music-studio
